SafeFace - Webcam Setup

 A webcam is required to take this test.

Setup Guide

  1. Allow Webcam Use

    Accept the prompt that appears informing that SafeFace wants permission to use/share/access your camera.

  2. Check Lighting Conditions

    The SafeFace test should be taken in a well-lit place free from glare.

  3. Get in position

    Make sure your entire face is captured in the preview to the left.
